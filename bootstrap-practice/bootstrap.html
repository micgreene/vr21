Black Tiger

Anime: Speed Racer
Driver: Prince Kim Jugger

The Black Tiger is a formidible rally car, featuring a fully sealed cockpit and specially modified brakes. The Black Tiger sees its fair share of tragedy however, as it is first blown up and restored, just to be lost to the desert quicksand.

Bonaparte

Anime: Dominion Tank Police
Driver: Officer Leona Ozaki

A miniature tank custom-built by Leona Ozaki and Al'Cu Ad Solte from parts of the Tiger-I Special after it was destroyed. Ran on spiked treads that would tear up the road beneath it and even featured a mechanism that allowed it to jump over obstacles.

Car Acrobatic Team

Anime: Speed Racer
Driver(lead): Captain Terror

A group of, as their name implies, automobile acrobats, as well as skilled racers. They are a recurring villainous organization. The Acrobatic Team is headed by Captain Terror, and their only other truly notable member is the cocky Snake Oiler.

Catbus

Anime: My Neighbor Totoro
Driver: Self-Driving

The Catbus is a transporting animal bus-looking creature. He is met by Mei, Satsuki, and Totoro. (though Totoro could have known him for years since he already knows to take it to get back to his home under the camper tree).

Griffon

Anime: Big O
Driver: Roger Smith

A full sized black luxury sedan comparable to a James Bond spy vehicle and the Batmobile. The vehicle has a noticeably long profile, akin to large American luxury sedans from the mid-20th century, specifically the late 1950s to late 1960s.

Kaneda's Bike

Anime: Akira
Driver: Shotaro Kaneda

Artist Katsuhiro Otomo has said, “In terms of Kaneda’s bike in Akira, the initial inspiration was the lightcycles from Tron designed by Syd Mead. However, they are wide, so I halved them and used that as an initial basis.”

Lancia Stratos

Anime: Ghost in the Shell
Driver: Batou Buttetsu

Batou is often seen driving a modified Lancia Stratos coupe, one of his most cherished possessions. Togusa uses Batou's love of the car as a comeback after being criticized for his use of a revolver instead of modern handgun.

Mach-5

Anime: Speed Racer
Driver: Speed Racer

An automotive machine incomparable to any other on the racing circuit. Unique aerodynamic design, coupled with special devices, six-speed manual transmission, and a top speed of ~250 mph.

Mammoth Car

Anime: Speed Racer
Driver: Crewed by Many

An 18' tall, over 200 yard-long, hulking single vehicle convoy. The Mammoth Car sports 44-wheels split among 10 different sections, the ability to maintain speeds of 200 MPH+ on any terrain, and electromagnetic brakes.

GRX

Anime: Speed Racer
Driver: Oriana Flub's subordinates

It is an extremely powerful and dangerous racing engine designed and built by automotive genius Ben Cranem. It could reach velocities so high that it negatively affects the driver's sense of coordination.

Fiat 500

Anime: Lupin III
Driver: Lupin III

The Fiat 500 (Italian: Cinquecento) is a rear-engined, four-seat, small city car that was manufactured and marketed by Fiat Automobiles from 1957 to 1975. In most of its appearances in Lupin III media, it is usually colored yellow.

Renalt 5 Turbo

Anime: Dragonball
Driver: Bulma Briefs

A 1984 Renault 5 Turbo, or R5 Turbo. Bulma's Car is destroyed by Son Goku while she's travelling through the Eastern Mountains, as he believed it to be some kind of monster. It's a Hoi-Poi Capsule car, and is quickly replaced by Bulma's Scooter.

RM02 "Mega-Max"

Anime: Promare
Driver: Lucia Fex

A mecha firetruck used during Burning Rescue operations. It stores various equipment under firefighter regulations and shoots out rescue gear from the ladder. It is piloted by Lucia Fex. The Mega Max's front features a plow for debris or other obstacles.

Trans Am 20000 WR

Anime: Redline
Driver: Sweet JP

A heavily customized Trans Am WR. It's implied it has already undergone extensive modification from the basic model to allow it to remain competitive by the time of the Yellowline race, the Trans Am might have been making well over 350,000 horsepower.

Shooting Star

Anime: Speed Racer
Driver: Racer X (Rex Racer)

Commissioned by Kapetapek Royal Industries, designed by Dr. Bent Cranium (the same man who had also designed the infamous GRX engine), and manufactured by Cranium Motor Works as the "9th Protégé Prototype" for use by Rex Racer.

Toyota AE86

Anime: Initial D
Driver: Takumi Fujiwara

Takumi Fujiwara's Toyota Sprinter Trueno GT-APEX (AE86), also known as The White Ghost of Akina, the Panda Trueno, or more simply as the Eight-Six (Hachi-Roku), is one of the main vehicles in the Initial D series.
